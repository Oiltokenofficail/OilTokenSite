🚀 Oil Token (OILTOKEN) — Project Roadmap

Strategic Development Phases

🚀 Phase 0 — Launch

This foundational phase ensures the deployment of the Jetton contract on the TON blockchain under full TIP-3 compliance. Metadata is published for transparency, and listings begin in major TON wallets. Initial liquidity is established on DEX platforms to allow early trading and strengthen community trust. Oil Token officially enters the ecosystem as a secure and verifiable asset.

👥 Phase 1 — Accessibility

Oil Token becomes widely accessible to the global community through listings on multiple TON-based DEXs. Social platforms including Telegram and X are activated to engage users. AMA sessions and guides are released to educate the first wave of holders. This stage builds awareness, accessibility, and trust among investors and the TON community.

🔗 Phase 2 — Adoption

Integration begins with partner Web3 projects and small-scale services. Payment tools and apps are built to facilitate real-world use. The project expands its partnerships and forms the foundation for real utility, connecting digital assets to physical energy markets through blockchain verification.

🌍 Phase 3 — Expansion

Oil Token expands globally by applying for centralized exchange (CEX) listings. Liquidity pools are enhanced, and smart contract audits ensure the highest standards of security. This stage establishes Oil Token as a recognized and sustainable global asset in both DeFi and traditional energy ecosystems.

💡 Phase 4 — Future Enhancements

The final phase focuses on innovation and long-term sustainability. Staking, DeFi integration, and cross-chain operations are implemented. Community governance enables holders to participate in decision-making. Strategic international partnerships will further align Oil Token with future energy technologies and blockchain advancements.
