Oil Token (OIL) - Tokenomics

Token Distribution & Allocation

Total Supply

The total supply of Oil Token is 10,000,000,000 OIL. No additional minting is possible.

Locked Supply (50%)

5,000,000,000 tokens are locked for 5 years. 1 billion tokens are unlocked every 2 years. Monthly distribution: 100 million tokens. Fully distributed in ~11 years and 3 months.

Initial Distribution

1,000,000,000 tokens will be distributed to Tonkeeper holders at launch.

Remaining Tokens (50%)

  • 10% (1,000,000,000) — Airdrops & Rewards
  • 20% (2,000,000,000) — Public Sale on Exchanges
  • 5% (500,000,000) — Core Team
  • 5% (500,000,000) — Community Development & Future Projects

Token Utility

Oil Token is a utility token representing 1 barrel of crude oil per token. It enables global access, secure transactions, and simplified asset tracking on the TON blockchain.
