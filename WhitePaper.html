Oil Token (OIL) — White Paper

Summary: Oil Token is a TIP-3 token on the TON blockchain, representing crude oil in digital form. Each token equals one barrel of oil, enabling secure, transparent, and global access.

Project Objective

To create a secure and transparent digital asset representing crude oil, accessible worldwide.

Key Features

  • Fixed supply: 10,000,000,000 tokens
  • No additional minting
  • Transparent and traceable transactions
  • Global accessibility
  • Easily transferable and verifiable
