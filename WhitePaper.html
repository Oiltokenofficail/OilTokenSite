Oil Token (OIL) - White Paper

Cover & Introduction

Oil Token (OIL) bridges the gap between digital assets and real-world crude oil, providing secure, transparent, and global access for users worldwide. Each token represents one barrel of crude oil, enabling seamless tracking, valuation, and investment.

Project Objective

The primary goal of Oil Token is to create a transparent and secure digital representation of crude oil. The project enables individuals, investors, and institutions to access oil assets without the traditional barriers of the physical market.

  • Global accessibility for all users
  • Blockchain-based transparency and security
  • Fixed and verifiable token supply

Problem & Opportunity

Challenges in Traditional Oil Market

  • Limited access for small-scale investors
  • Legal and international complexities
  • Lack of transparency in transactions

Opportunities with Digital Oil

  • Tokenization of crude oil assets
  • Blockchain-based tracking and verification
  • Global access to oil investments

Token Details

Oil Token is implemented as a TIP-3 token on the TON blockchain with the following specifications:

  • Name: Oil Token
  • Symbol: OIL
  • Decimals: 9
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 OIL
  • Fixed Supply: No additional minting

Tokenomics & Distribution

The distribution of Oil Tokens is carefully structured to ensure fairness, decentralization, and community growth:

  • Locked Supply (50%): 5,000,000,000 tokens locked for 11 years, gradually released.
  • Initial Distribution: 1,000,000,000 tokens allocated to Tonkeeper holders.
  • Remaining Tokens (4,000,000,000): Divided among Airdrops, Public Sale, Core Team, and Community Development.

Roadmap

  • Phase 0 - Launch: Deploy Jetton contract, publish metadata, list in TON wallets, initial DEX liquidity.
  • Phase 1 - Accessibility: List on major DEX, activate community channels, attract early holders, AMA sessions.
  • Phase 2 - Adoption: Partnerships with Web3 projects, integrate into services, build simple payment tools.
  • Phase 3 - Expansion: Strengthen liquidity, apply for CEX listings, smart contract audits.

Exchange & Trading

Users can buy OIL using TON. Currently, the system displays token calculation and payment instructions for the owner’s wallet. Full trading features will be enabled after exchange listing.

Security & Transparency

All transactions and token ownership are recorded on the TON blockchain. The Jetton contract ensures:

  • Immutable and verifiable ownership
  • Transparent token distribution
  • Auditability by the community and developers
