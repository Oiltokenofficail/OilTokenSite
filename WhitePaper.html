Oil (Oiltoken) — Whitepaper

Energy-backed token on the TON blockchain — Official documentation (2025)

Cover & Executive Summary

Oil (Oiltoken) bridges digital assets and real-world crude oil by tokenizing oil-value on the TON blockchain. Each token represents the digital economic value tied to crude oil. Oiltoken enables transparent trading, tracking, and participation in energy markets — designed for global accessibility, security, and long-term stability.

Project Objective

The primary objective of Oil is to create a secure, transparent, and accessible digital representation of crude oil that opens energy markets to retail and institutional users worldwide. The project reduces intermediaries and provides blockchain-native ownership of energy value.

  • Global accessibility for retail and institutional investors
  • Transparency and auditability via TON blockchain
  • Fixed, verifiable token supply and predictable release schedule

Market Problem & Opportunity

Challenges in Traditional Oil Markets

  • Limited access for small-scale investors
  • Complex legal and cross-border constraints
  • Opaqueness and reliance on intermediaries
  • High costs and slow settlement

Opportunity

Tokenization of crude oil creates instant, permissionless access to oil-backed assets, improving liquidity and transparency. By representing oil value on-chain, Oiltoken unlocks a large addressable market within energy and DeFi ecosystems.

Solution Overview

Oiltoken tokenizes crude oil value via TIP-3 (Jetton) tokens on the TON network. The smart contract model ensures transparent supply rules, verifiable ownership, and on-chain transferability. Investors can trade Oiltoken on DEXs (like Ston.fi), participate in staking and rewards, and access energy-backed dApps.

Tokenomics & Distribution

Distribution is structured to ensure fair community participation, liquidity, and long-term project stability.

  • Locked Supply: 50% — 5,000,000,000 Oiltoken (team/project reserve with staged vesting)
  • Initial Distribution (Tonkeeper holders): 10% — 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Public Sale / Exchanges: 20% — 2,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Airdrops & Rewards: 10% — 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Core Team: 5% — 500,000,000 Oiltoken (subject to vesting)
  • Community & Development: 5% — 500,000,000 Oiltoken

Vesting & Release Pattern (Locked Supply):

  • Cliff Period: 60 months (5 years) — no tokens released during cliff.
  • After the first 5 years, 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken are released over 10 months (100,000,000 / month).
  • Remaining locked supply follows the same pattern: after each tranche release, the next tranche remains locked for 2 years, then the next 1B is released over 10 months.
  • Total locked distribution completes in approximately ~16 years and 3 months (accounting for cliffs and staged releases).

Supply & Circulating Supply

Total Supply: 10,000,000,000 Oiltoken (no additional minting).
Circulating Supply (current): tokens not locked or vested. At launch, initial distribution to Tonkeeper holders (10%) and public allocations are considered circulating; locked supply (5B) is excluded until vesting releases occur. For live numbers, refer to the Tokenomics page and block explorer.

Release Schedule

Frequency: Monthly (during release windows).
First unlock: After 60-month cliff, monthly releases of 100,000,000 Oiltoken for 10 months (first 1B). Subsequent tranches follow every 2 years by the same monthly schedule.

What Makes Oil Unique

Oiltoken is commodity-backed (crude oil) and built on TON for fast, low-cost transactions. The project combines long-term vesting discipline, transparent on-chain accounting, and a team with both energy-market and blockchain experience to enable real-world integrations and sustainable growth.

Revenue Model

Revenue streams include transaction fees, staking commissions, liquidity pool yields, partnership revenues, and service integrations. A portion of revenue is allocated to community development, ecosystem growth, and future strategic initiatives.

Security & Transparency

All token transactions and balances are recorded on the TON blockchain. The TIP-3 Jetton contract enforces supply rules and transfer logic. The project will publish on-chain proof, explorer links, and pursue third-party audits as appropriate to ensure trust and verifiability.
