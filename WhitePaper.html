Oil Token (OIL) — White Paper

Digital Gateway to Crude Oil — TIP-3 on TON

Summary

Oil Token is a TIP-3 token on the TON blockchain. Each token represents one barrel of crude oil. The goal is to provide a secure, transparent and accessible digital representation of oil assets for global users.

1. Project Objective

To create a secure and transparent digital asset representing real crude oil, accessible worldwide and transferable with simple verification on the TON blockchain.

2. Key Features

  • Fixed supply: 10,000,000,000 OIL
  • No further minting: ownership fully released, supply fixed
  • Traceability: all token movements verifiable on-chain
  • Global access: anyone with TON wallet can receive OIL

3. Problem & Opportunity

Traditional oil markets are centralized and often inaccessible for small traders. Tokenization offers improved transparency, fractional access and simplified cross-border transfers.

4. How It Works

OIL is implemented as a TIP-3 Jetton on TON. Token metadata and contract are published publicly. 1 OIL = 1 barrel (conceptual representation). Ownership transfers use normal TON wallets and Jetton transfer calls.

5. Roadmap

  • Phase 0 — Launch: Deploy Jetton contract, publish metadata, list in TON wallets, provide initial DEX liquidity.
  • Phase 1 — Accessibility: List on major DEX platforms, activate community channels, run AMAs.
  • Phase 2 — Adoption: Integrations with Web3 services and payment tools.
  • Phase 3 — Expansion: Strengthen liquidity and seek CEX listings, perform audits.
