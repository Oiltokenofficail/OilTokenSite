Oil Token (OILTOKEN) — Whitepaper

Energy-backed token on the TON blockchain — Gold + Purple edition

Cover & Introduction

Oil Token (OILTOKEN) bridges digital assets and real-world crude oil, providing secure, transparent, and global access. Each token represents one barrel of crude oil. OILTOKEN enables seamless tracking, valuation, and participation in energy markets through the TON blockchain.

Project Objective

The primary objective is to create a transparent, secure, and accessible digital representation of crude oil. Oil Token opens energy markets to a broader audience by reducing intermediaries and offering blockchain-native ownership.

  • Global accessibility for retail and institutional users
  • Blockchain-based transparency and auditability
  • Fixed and verifiable token supply

Problem & Market Opportunity

Challenges in Traditional Oil Markets

  • Limited access for small-scale investors
  • Regulatory and cross-border complexities
  • Lack of transparency and high intermediary costs

Opportunity

Tokenization enables instant, permissionless access to oil-backed assets, increasing liquidity and transparency. The global energy market presents a significant opportunity for digital asset integration.

Security & Transparency

All token activities are recorded on TON. The Jetton (TIP-3) smart contract ensures immutability and verifiability. Audits, public explorer links, and on-chain records are used to maintain trust.

What Makes Oil Token Unique

OIL is commodity-backed (oil), built on TON for low-cost, fast transactions, with a long-term vesting structure to preserve stability. The hybrid team expertise in energy and blockchain provides a practical edge for real-world integrations.

Revenue Model

Revenue sources include transaction and staking fees, liquidity pool yields, partnerships, and service integrations. A portion of revenues supports community programs and future development.
