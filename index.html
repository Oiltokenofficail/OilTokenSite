About Oilgram

Oilgram is a P2P voice-call subsystem inside the OILToken platform. Users register, choose a 10-digit number starting with 666, and make secure P2P calls.

  1. Sign up with email & password (verification required).
  2. Choose a number starting with 666.
  3. Use Oilgram page to create or answer calls.
  4. Number marketplace opens after free period.
