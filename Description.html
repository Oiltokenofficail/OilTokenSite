The Oiltoken represents a fixed-supply energy-backed digital asset on the TON blockchain. No further minting will ever occur, ensuring supply scarcity and transparent tokenomics verified on-chain.

Distribution Model

The distribution is designed to guarantee fairness, ecosystem sustainability, and long-term stability.

  • Locked Supply: 50% — 5,000,000,000 Oiltoken (team/project reserve with multi-year vesting)
  • Initial Distribution (Tonkeeper Holders): 10% — 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Public Sale / Exchange Liquidity: 20% — 2,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Airdrops & Rewards: 10% — 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken
  • Core Team: 5% — 500,000,000 Oiltoken (subject to vesting)
  • Community & Development: 5% — 500,000,000 Oiltoken

Vesting & Lockup Policy

Total Locked Supply: 5,000,000,000 Oiltoken

  • Cliff Period: 60 months (5 years) — no tokens released during this period.
  • After the first 5 years, 1,000,000,000 Oiltoken will be unlocked gradually over 10 months (100,000,000 per month).
  • Each subsequent tranche remains locked for 2 years before the next 1B unlock cycle begins.
  • Total locked distribution completes in approximately 16 years and 3 months, ensuring long-term commitment and market stability.

Circulating Supply

At launch, 2,000,000,000 Oiltoken (Public + Airdrops) and 1,000,000,000 distributed to Tonkeeper holders make up the initial circulation. The 5B locked supply remains excluded until vesting release events. Real-time circulating supply can be monitored through the Ton blockchain explorer and official dashboards.

Release Schedule

  • Frequency: Monthly, during release periods.
  • First Unlock: After 60-month cliff.
  • Monthly Release: 100,000,000 Oiltoken for 10 months (first tranche).
  • Subsequent unlocks follow the same 10-month pattern every 2 years.
  • Completion: Entire 5B locked supply distributed over ~16.25 years.

Utility & Value Creation

  • Trading on decentralized exchanges such as Ston.fi.
  • Participation in energy-backed dApps and DeFi products.
  • Governance, staking, and community reward systems.
  • Access to a stable, oil-backed asset class for global users.

Liquidity & Exchanges

Liquidity pools are deployed primarily on decentralized exchanges (DEX) within TON’s ecosystem. Additional listings on centralized exchanges (CEX) are planned as the project matures.

Example Pool: Ston.fi — Oiltoken Liquidity Pool

Transparency & Verification

All token balances, transactions, and vesting contracts are recorded publicly on the TON blockchain. The smart contract is open-source, audited, and available for verification through TonViewer.
